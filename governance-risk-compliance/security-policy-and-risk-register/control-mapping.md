# Control Mapping

I created this control map to practice connecting security work to recognized control language.

| Security Activity | NIST CSF Function | Example Evidence | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Asset inventory | Identify | Asset list with owners | Helps prioritize protection and remediation |
| MFA enforcement | Protect | MFA policy export | Reduces account takeover risk |
| Log monitoring | Detect | SIEM alert and query evidence | Helps identify suspicious behavior |
| Incident triage | Respond | Incident report and timeline | Supports consistent response and handoff |
| Backup validation | Recover | Restore test evidence | Supports recovery after disruption |
| Access reviews | Protect | Review signoff and exceptions | Helps maintain least privilege |
